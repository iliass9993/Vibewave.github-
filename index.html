To create a user account, click on the "Sign Up" button at the top right corner of the homepage. Fill out the form with your personal information, including your name, email address, and password. You will receive a confirmation email to finalize your registration.

To add songs to a playlist, navigate to the song you want to add. Click on the "Add to Playlist" icon next to the song. Select the playlist you want to add the song to or create a new playlist.

Currently, our music library service is online only. You need to be connected to the Internet to listen to songs. However, we are working on an offline feature that will be available soon.

To contact technical support, click on "Contact" in the footer of the site. Fill out the contact form with your question or issue, and our support team will get back to you as soon as possible.

If you want to delete your account, go to your account settings. Click on "Delete My Account" and confirm your choice. Please note that this action is irreversible and all your data, including your playlists, will be permanently deleted.
